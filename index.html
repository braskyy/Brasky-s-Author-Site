                        <h2 class="title">A HIDEOUS ECSTASY</h2>

                        <p class="genre">A Novel</p>

                        

                        <div class="copy">

                            <p>Step into a digital abyss where every thumb-swipe sucks you deeper into a carnivalesque oubliette. In the humid purgatory of Southernesse, Carolina, municipal procurement analyst Dwayne Ferris is lonely, heartbroken, and disillusioned. When he attempts to &ldquo;optimize&rdquo; his life with a discount brain-hacking machine, he unwittingly triggers a descent into radical extremism, fixating on the destruction of Hinge&rsquo;s CEO and the &ldquo;Cop City&rdquo; training center.<p>

As Dwayne unravels, his obsession entangles an ensemble of characters navigating their own modern derangements: Heather, a sun-staring revolutionary poet and creator of &ldquo;The Boyfriend Blockchain;&rdquo; Tammy, a cigarette-ripping geriatric mechanic evading a dark past; Suraj, a convenience-store clerk grappling with grief and frayed faith; Johntay, a man clinging to sanity amidst the monotony of a call center; Connor, inventor of &ldquo;Seen Screen,&rdquo; trying to save young men one PornHub tab at a time; Stephanie, an artist building the world&rsquo;s first 24-room tarot installation; and Jimmy, a suicidal punk rocker who hangs around longer than expected.<p>

From tranquil forests to blood-stained sidewalks and supermax cells, A Hideous Ecstasy traverses the landscapes and screenscapes of a fractured society. It depicts a world where everything is play and amusement—right up until a liquescent, gore-lacquered reality crawls from beneath your Toyota Camry and knocks on your door.</p>
